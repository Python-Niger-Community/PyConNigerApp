Python Niger Community Health & Safety Policy

The health and safety of our participants, staff, and community are our top priorities. The Python Niger Community events are committed to implementing measures to prevent the spread of COVID-19 and ensuring a safe and secure environment for all attendees. This policy outlines the guidelines and protocols that will be in place throughout the event.

* General Guidelines *

1. Vaccination Requirement

All attendees are strongly encouraged to be fully vaccinated against COVID-19 before attending the event. Proof of vaccination may be required for entry.

2. Health Screenings

Attendees are expected to conduct self-health assessments before arriving at the event. Individuals experiencing symptoms such as fever, cough, or difficulty breathing are requested to stay home.

3. Mask Mandate

Masks must be worn at all times during the event, except when actively eating or drinking. Attendees are responsible for bringing their own masks, but a limited supply will be available at the venue.

4. Hand Hygiene

Hand sanitizer stations will be placed throughout the venue. Attendees are encouraged to practice frequent handwashing and use hand sanitizer regularly.

5. Social Distancing

Participants are required to maintain physical distancing in accordance with local health guidelines. Seating arrangements, session layouts, and common areas will be organized to facilitate social distancing.

6. Contactless Check-In

Attendees are encouraged to use contactless methods for registration and check-in processes. Digital badges and materials will be provided.

* Venue Safety Measures *

Ventilation

The venue will ensure adequate ventilation in all event spaces. Where possible, outdoor spaces will be utilized for activities.

1. Sanitization Protocols

Enhanced cleaning and sanitization measures will be implemented throughout the event, with a focus on high-touch surfaces and shared equipment.

2. First Aid and Medical Assistance

Trained medical personnel will be available on-site to provide assistance if needed. First aid stations will be clearly marked.

3. Emergency Evacuation Procedures

Participants will be briefed on emergency evacuation procedures, and venue staff will be available to assist in case of an emergency.

* Communication and Updates *

1. Health and Safety Announcements

Regular announcements and reminders about health and safety measures will be made throughout the event.

2. Event Updates

Attendees will be informed promptly of any changes to the event schedule or protocols through official event communication channels.

3. Participant Cooperation

All attendees are expected to cooperate with health and safety protocols to ensure a secure and comfortable environment for everyone.

By attending the Python Niger Community events, participants agree to comply with this COVID/Health & Safety Policy. The organizers reserve the right to update and modify these guidelines as needed based on the evolving public health situation.
