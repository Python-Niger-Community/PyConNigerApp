Speaking at PyCon Niger 2024: Detailed Information

Introduction

PyCon Niger 2024 is an exceptional platform for Python enthusiasts, developers, and industry professionals to share their knowledge and insights. We welcome speakers from all backgrounds and levels of experience to contribute to this vibrant event. Below are the details on how you can participate as a speaker, including the registration process, proposal submissions, lightning talks, and workshops.

Speaker Proposals

We invite proposals for a variety of session types:

  • * Talks:
    • Duration: 30 to 45 minutes.
    • Content: Share your expertise on a specific topic related to Python. This could include case studies, research findings, industry insights, or advanced technical content.
    • Requirements: Provide a detailed abstract (150-300 words), an outline of the talk, and key takeaways for the audience.
  • * Lightning Talks:
    • Duration: 5 to 10 minutes.
    • Content: Short, engaging presentations on interesting topics. Ideal for sharing quick insights, project overviews, or introducing new ideas.
    • Requirements: Provide a brief description (50-100 words) and main points to be covered.
  • * Workshops:
    • Duration: 1 to 2 hours.
    • Content: Interactive, hands-on sessions where participants can engage in practical exercises. Topics can range from beginner to advanced levels.
    • Requirements: Submit a comprehensive outline, including prerequisites for participants, materials required, and a step-by-step breakdown of the workshop activities.

Proposal Submission Guidelines

  • Original Content: Ensure that your talk or workshop content is original and not previously presented at major conferences.
  • Relevance: Focus on topics that are relevant to the Python community and PyCon Nigerâ€™s audience.
  • Clarity: Write clear and concise proposals. Avoid jargon and make sure the objectives and benefits are easy to understand.
  • Diversity: We encourage submissions from speakers of diverse backgrounds, including those from underrepresented groups in technology.

Selection Process

The selection process includes:

  • Review: All proposals will be reviewed by the PyCon Niger 2024 Program Committee.
  • Evaluation: Proposals will be evaluated based on relevance, clarity, originality, and potential impact on the audience.
  • Notification: Selected speakers will be notified via email and provided with further instructions and support to prepare for their sessions.

Benefits for Speakers

  • Free Conference Pass: Speakers receive a complimentary pass to attend PyCon Niger 2024.
  • Networking Opportunities: Engage with other experts, industry leaders, and enthusiasts.
  • Exposure: Gain visibility within the Python community and beyond through our event promotions.

Important Dates

  • Proposal Submission Deadline: July 30, 2024
  • Notification of Acceptance: August 14, 2024
  • Conference Dates: 28-29 September, 2024

Contact Information

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please contact us at contact@pythonniger.org or visit our website www.pythonniger.org.

Join us at PyCon Niger 2024 and share your passion for Python with a dynamic and enthusiastic community!
