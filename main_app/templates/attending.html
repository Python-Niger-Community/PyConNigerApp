PyCon Niger Registration Categories

Here are the different types of registration categories for PyCon Niger:

  1. 1. Corporate Registration (25,000xof):

    This category is tailored for companies or organizations sending employees to attend PyCon Niger.

    Corporate registration packages may include bulk discounts for multiple attendees from the same organization.

    Participants under this category typically have access to all conference sessions, workshops, and networking events.

  2. 2. Individual Registration (20,000xof):

    Individual registration is suitable for professionals, freelancers, or enthusiasts attending PyCon Niger on their own.

    This category provides access to all conference sessions, workshops, and networking events.

    Individuals may have the option to choose add-ons or upgrades based on their preferences.

  3. 3. Student Registration (15,000xof):

    Student registration is designed for students enrolled in educational institutions who wish to attend PyCon Niger.

    This category often offers discounted rates to make the conference more accessible to students.

    Student registrants may need to provide valid student identification to qualify for the discounted rate.

  4. 4. Children Registration (10,000xof):

    Children registration is specifically for young attendees accompanying their parents or guardians to PyCon Niger.

    This category may offer reduced or complimentary registration fees for children below a certain age.

    Child registrants may have access to select sessions or activities tailored for their age group.
