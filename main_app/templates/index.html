PyCon Niger

Is the premier annual national technology conference organized by Python Niger Community, bringing together Python enthusiasts, developers, and industry professionals from across Niger Republic. As the largest gathering of its kind in the country, PyCon Niger serves as a platform for collaboration, innovation, and knowledge-sharing within the Python community. Attendees have the opportunity to participate in a wide range of activities, including keynote presentations, technical workshops, panel discussions, and networking events. Through PyCon Niger, participants are empowered to explore the latest trends in Python programming, exchange ideas, and contribute to the advancement of technology in Niger Republic and beyond.
